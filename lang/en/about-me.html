What is 42?

42 is a unique programming school founded by Xavier Niel. It offers a tuition-free computer science education with no traditional teachers or lectures. The curriculum is based on project-based learning and a peer-to-peer method, where students learn by collaborating with one another. The program is intensive and does not require any prior degrees. 42 stands out for its innovative approach and its ability to train skilled, adaptable developers ready to tackle modern technological challenges.
